Pierre Calmels

My name is Pierre. I was born in New Milford, Connecticut" on 11/11/1980. My employment history consists of some varied and interesting experinces. My most gainful employment thus far has been as a bartender, working at some great places including; The Pequot Inn, The Mayflower Inn, The Roger Sherman Inn, The Cobb's Mill Inn, and Le Chateau Restaurant. My experiences at these places were formative and lasting. I've served some of the rich and the imfamous, but the highlight of my career wasn't at one of these places, but at the downstairs bar of Harry's New York Bar, Paris where I served water to a cat. It was downhill from there. I've also had temporary roles at UBS Investment Bank in the Legal Department, and also in the Wealth Management Unit. I once took a summer job working for a movie catering company on such films as; Old Dogs, College Road Trip, and Indiana Jones and the Cystal Skull. Something I believe strongly in is finding something to learn from every person I meet, and from every experience. Something that I've learned from each of these jobs is that the lowest positions offer a perspective much richer than the paygrade. My formal education has been completed in fits and starts, beginning with Naugatuck Valley Community College where I earned an Associate's degree in Business Finance and culminating Ball State University -- GO Cardinals! -- where I earned a Bachelor's in General Studies. For the past three years while finishing my bachelor's I have been driving an Uber through-out Connecticut. Out of necessity I learned how to fix my car, mostly from You Tube videos and then getting my hands and everything else, dirty. And in the past two months I have started learning to code, one of the most challenging things I have ever done, and I hope it will also be one of the most rewarding.
